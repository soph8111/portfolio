Bike Configurator

This bike configurator was a school project on third semester. Together with my team we made a prototype in Adobe XD. My main focus in this project was to separate and edit all the different bike parts in photoshop and export them in illustrator as a SVG file. Also, I did the styling with CSS and made the animations with gsap.
